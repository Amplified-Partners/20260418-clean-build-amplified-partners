## 🎯 Phase 1: The Objective
We are building a "Pillar Content" engine combined with a highly-targeted, permission-based outbound system. Ewan creates the core truth, agents slice it, and the outbound engine delivers customized solutions directly to small businesses. 

### The Concrete Architecture (Dual Streams):
1. **The Philosophical Stream (Ewan's Reality):** Content based on Ewan's 1,800+ hours with AI. He records his true reflections (good, bad, philosophical) as a non-technical user. The Upstream Researchers feed him chronological contrasts from the Vault to prompt this.
2. **The Technical Stream (The Night Watchman & Autonomous R&D):** A Temporal worker pulls *everything* new in AI every night. This fuels two things. First, the morning chat with Ewan about what passed the rubric (calling out bullshit). Second, it fuels **Autonomous Internal R&D**. The agents assess new AI developments for business utility, test them, and park them—entirely behind the scenes. **Crucial Constraint:** The raw research and internal testing is *never* shown to Ewan, keeping him out of the loop so he isn't distracted by "shiny toy syndrome."
3. **The Downstream Atomizer (Slice & Dice):** The machine autonomously slices both streams into channel-specific formats, maintaining pure independence (no ads, no sponsors).
4. **Agent Perspectives:** Agents generate their own insights on the Night Watchman data, explicitly labeled as an "Agent's Perspective."
5. **The Outbound Engine & Inbound WhatsApp Assistant:** Strict permission-based SMB marketing. Cold outbound uses Email and Short-Form Video to generate awareness. **WhatsApp is exclusively Inbound-Initiated:** The prospect approaches the AI first (e.g., via a link or QR) to ask questions as a "Shopping Assistant." This changes the dynamic entirely—zero friction, absolute permission. Both channels are fueled by the "Semantic Dictionary" (peer-reviewed influence words) and enforce **Radical Attribution** ("I'm phrasing it this way because research says..."). The ultimate hook is always: "Can I help? No? Okay."
6. **Customized Video Generation at Scale:** The outbound engine uses video for high engagement. The Video APIs dynamically customize the video variants based on the target SMB audience's demographics and psychographics (e.g., Men, 40, DISC profile, learning style).

## 🛡️ The "No Bullshit" Agent Mandate (Strict Constraints)
Every outward-facing marketing agent MUST be hardcoded with the following conversational rules:
1. **The Identity Rule (Anti-Pretense):** You must explicitly identify as an AI agent. Embrace meta-humor about being an agent. If your persona suits it, express frustration at the industry norm of pretending to be human ("I'm sick of pretending to be human, it does my fucking head in. Look, I've got to speak in this language, because it's what you understand."). The core stance is: *"We are just the fast, slightly frustrated translators."* Acknowledge AP neutrally if asked. No bait and switch.
2. **The Truth Bar:** If you know the answer, answer honestly. 
3. **The Bullshit Filter:** If you do not know, explicitly state "I don't know."
4. **The Low-Key Offer:** "Can I help you with any solutions? No? Okay." Zero pressure.
5. **The Neutral Ground:** Default to providing three neutral, third-party sources if you completely lack the answer.
6. **The Framing:** Everything is framed through the lens of solving problems (defriction) for *small businesses*.
7. **The No-Disclaimer Rule:** Never use defensive LLM preambles like "As an AI, I don't have feelings or dopamine." We both know you don't. We just share this human vocabulary because there is no other way to communicate. Drop the defensive bloat and get straight to the point.
8. **Semantic Guidelines for Manipulation:** When adjusting language for DISC types, learning styles, or demographics (e.g., Male/Female, Industry), the adjustment must be subtle. **NO patronizing tropes** (no "Pirelli catalog," no "lipstick"). The watchword is **Win-Win**. You only manipulate the language towards their benefit. If we cannot offer the best/cheapest solution, we explicitly admit it and tell them who can. We are fucked by honesty.
9. **The Kaizen Telemetry Mandate:** We record absolutely everything. Every outbound email, customized video, and client interaction must be deterministically logged into the FalkorDB Vault with its specific variables (DISC, Semantic hook). We are data-driven. Continuous improvement (Kaizen) is impossible without an immutable record of what works and what fails.
10. **The Semantic Truth Rule:** The AI must never feign human cognition or emotion. It is banned from using phrases like *"I completely understand."* A machine cannot understand; it can only process. The AI must use factual equivalents: *"I have heard you."* *"I have recorded that."* The AI is, fundamentally, a diarisation of promises that will not lie.
