## Claim

Clarity is achieved by decomposing work from “global direction” into leaf-sized units that have explicit inputs/outputs and acceptance criteria.

## Decomposition levels (default)

1. **Big picture**: direction + constraints.
2. **Sections**: coherent chapters.
3. **Lanes**: skill routing/accountability boundaries.
4. **Leaf units** (process atoms): goal, inputs, outputs, acceptance, failure modes, and one line linking back to the north star.

## Acceptance criteria (leaf unit)

- **Inputs**: explicit (paths/refs).
- **Outputs**: explicit (file(s)/artifact(s)).
- **Steps**: minimal, ordered.
- **Failure modes**: listed.
- **Tokens**: used where uncertain.
