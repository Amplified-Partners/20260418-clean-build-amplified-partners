# Death Spiral Detection (Early Warning System)

**Purpose:** Monitor for 6 early warning patterns indicating catastrophic business failure risk.

## The 6 Warning Patterns

| Category | Indicator | Action Trigger |
|----------|-----------|----------------|
| Financial | Altman Z < 1.81 | Immediate alert |
| Liquidity | Deteriorating CCC | Trend analysis |
| Operational | Constraint misidentified | Goldratt re-analysis |
| Market | Customer churn spike | Growth pivot |
| Behavioral | Owner decision paralysis | Intervention |
| Systemic | Multi-point failure cascade | Emergency protocol |

## Detection Discipline

- **Always-on monitoring** of client financial streams
- **6-month advance notice** target (alert before collapse)
- **Threshold-based** — no human judgment required
- **Automated response** — triggers recovery rubrik immediately

## Output

Alert to client + recovery pathway activation via DANGER-SPIRAL-EXIT rubrik.
