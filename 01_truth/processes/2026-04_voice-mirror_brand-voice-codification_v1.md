# VOICE_MIRROR.md (Brand Voice Constitutional Document)

**Purpose:** Capture, codify, and enforce client brand voice for AI-generated content.

## Layer 1 Constitutional Status

VOICE_MIRROR.md is **Layer 1 immutable** for content agents — loaded before any task context, cannot be overridden.

## Structure

```markdown
# VOICE_MIRROR.md — [Client Name] Brand Voice

## Vocabulary Norms
- Words we use: [list]
- Words we never use: [list]
- Industry terms: [acceptable context]

## Tone Spectrum
- Minimum warmth: [1-10 scale]
- Maximum formality: [1-10 scale]
- Prohibited tones: [list]

## Sentence Structure
- Average length: [short/medium/long]
- Complexity ceiling: [grade level]
- Rhythm patterns: [punchy/flowing/balanced]

## What to Avoid
- Prohibited phrases: [list]
- Competitor language: [list]
- Jargon categories: [list]
```

## Process

1. Interview client on tone, vocabulary, style preferences
2. Analyze existing content samples for patterns
3. Write VOICE_MIRROR.md constitutional document
4. Client review and approval
5. Inject as Layer 1 of every content agent's prompt
6. Test generation and score voice consistency (0-10)
7. Iterate until passing threshold

## Verification

Score test content against VOICE_MIRROR.md. Below threshold → revise. Above threshold → publish.
