# Shadow Month (Zero-Intervention Baseline)

**Purpose:** Establish validated baseline of client operations before any automation or intervention.

## The Rule

> **30 days of passive observation. Zero AI interventions.**

## Process

1. Connect to client accounting software (Xero/Sage/QuickBooks)
2. Configure passive monitoring — observe patterns, collect data, identify friction
3. **Zero interventions** during 30-day observation period
4. Run Financial Autopsy (Altman Z + Goldratt ToC + CCC)
5. Map operations against 16-Point Friction Taxonomy
6. Generate AMPS scores per identified process
7. Compile baseline report: AMPS scores, friction inventory, Financial Autopsy results
8. Present to client at end of Month 1
9. Client approves improvement roadmap before any automation begins

## Why This Matters

Without validated baseline:
- Cannot measure improvement accurately
- Client doesn't trust AI recommendations
- Automation targets wrong friction points

## Output

Validated baseline report + approved improvement roadmap.
