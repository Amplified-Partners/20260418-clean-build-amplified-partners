# Financial Autopsy (Three-Rubric Assessment)

**Purpose:** Deliver plain-language financial health assessment using deterministic rubrics.

## The Three Rubrics

| Rubric | Formula | Interpretation |
|--------|---------|----------------|
| **Altman Z-Score** | Z = 1.2(X1) + 1.4(X2) + 3.3(X3) + 0.6(X4) + 1.0(X5) | Z<1.81: distress, 1.81-2.99: grey, >2.99: safe |
| **Goldratt Theory of Constraints** | Five Focusing Steps | Identify #1 constraint limiting growth |
| **Cash Conversion Cycle** | CCC = DIO + DSO - DPO | Liquidity health indicator |

## When to Use

- Client connects accounting software (onboarding)
- Continuous monitoring for paid tiers
- Death spiral early warning detection

## Output

Plain-language report. Business owner reads without accounting knowledge.
