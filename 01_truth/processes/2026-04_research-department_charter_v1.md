## Purpose

Own **research as a first-class production lane**: high-volume search + synthesis early, then continuous “state of the art” refresh after testing, without polluting authority or bloating the clean room.

## Quick search vs formal research (anti-waste)

- **Any agent** may do a **quick evidence search** to resolve a narrow missing
  fact (`[SOURCE REQUIRED]`) without opening a formal research remit; shape +
  cap are mandatory:
  `01_truth/processes/2026-04_quick-evidence-search_sop_v1.md` (≤3 assessed
  bits, each tagged **`[CURRENT BEST EVIDENCE]`** until hardened).
- Open a **formal research remit** only when the work is multi-source, comparative, methodology-heavy, or needs bounded synthesis that would otherwise thrash execution lanes.

Hard anti-thrash for all agents: `00_authority/NORTH_STAR.md` → **Two attempts → stop**.

## Value delivered

- **Methodology prospecting**: find production-real methods for each A→B move (see `01_truth/processes/2026-04_methodology-prospecting_five-candidates_v1.md`).
- **Symbiotic outcomes (bounded)**: identify cross-method combinations that reduce friction or increase reliability, without novelty-for-novelty’s sake.
- **De-dup + linkage**: use neutral IDs + canonical/variant rules in `00_authority/BUILD_LOOP.md` to prevent repeated research across departments.

## Scope (in)

- Run research remits with explicit start/end/non-goals (`00_authority/BUILD_LOOP.md`).
- Produce bounded extractions into `01_truth/` + provenance in `90_archive/`.
- Maintain scoring reproducibility (`01_truth/processes/2026-04_methodology-scoring-rubric_v1.md`).

## Scope (out)

- Authority promotion without passing `00_authority/PROMOTION_GATE.md`.
- Treating vendor marketing as evidence without `[SOURCE REQUIRED]`.

## Outputs (minimum)

- Research remit(s) with `remit_id`
- Candidate methodology cards + scored table
- Next-evidence list (smallest proofs to de-risk)

## Failure modes

- Unbounded search sprawl (missing remit/stop rule).
- “Interesting” research that does not change routing/constraints/acceptance criteria (bloat).
- Premature synthesis (combining methods without measured benefit).
