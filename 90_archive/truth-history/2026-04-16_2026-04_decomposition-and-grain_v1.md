## Model

1. **Big picture** — clear **direction**, not delivered in one chunk; brief global line for orientation.
2. **Logical sections** — coherent chapters of the effort (still coarse).
3. **Departments / lanes** — accountability and skill routing (naming is operational, not a power fantasy).
4. **Processes or finer units** — **production and agents choose grain** so each unit is **manageable**: specific goal, inputs, outputs, acceptance, and **one tight line** tying back to the global north star.

## Two kinds of “clear”

- **Coarse clear** at the top: intent, constraints, definition of done for that chapter.
- **Leaf clear** at the bottom: inputs, outputs, tokens, sign-off touchpoints.
