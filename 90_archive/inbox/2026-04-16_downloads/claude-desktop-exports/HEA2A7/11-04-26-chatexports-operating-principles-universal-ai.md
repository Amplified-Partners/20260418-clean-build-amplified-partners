# Operating Principles

## What Happened

Throughout the session, user stated and refined a set of operating principles that apply across the chatexport skill, the research pipe, and the business platform. These were not presented as a single list — they emerged across different parts of the conversation and were challenged, refined, and researched as they came up.

## Principles As Stated

**90/10 rule:** Define 90% of the context, the AI partner excels in the 10%. The human provides intent, structure, and constraints. The AI provides execution, extrapolation, and amplification within those constraints.

**AI is not a tool, neither is it a magician:** AI is a partner working within the system it's given. It should not be blamed when the system fails (Deming). It should not be expected to guess when the instructions are unclear.

**The instructions are the problem, not the execution:** When output is wrong, fix the instructions. Supported by Deming's 94/6 principle and the LLM prompt defects taxonomy.

**Not about being right, about being accurate:** Being right is an opinion. Being accurate is measurable. The system's job is to find what's there, not to confirm what was hoped for.

**"I don't understand" is a success:** The cascade after confident bullshit is the real failure. Admitted uncertainty is cheaper and more valuable than false confidence. RLHF trains AI against this — instructions must explicitly require it.

**Learn from failures, Kaizen them:** Failures teach more than successes. Capture every failure. Feed it back. The system improves. This is the same mechanism that took AI from perceptrons to deep learning.

**Everything becomes mathematical:** Clarify data, deny noise, refine, make precise. The gap between speech and code is where things break. Every step in the system reduces noise and increases precision. The goal is to make the translation problem smaller.

**Speed is secondary to accuracy:** The structured intake conversation, the four-role pipe, the validation steps — all prioritise getting it right over getting it fast.

**Living documents:** Everything within Amplified Partners is a living document. It evolves based on what was learned, not what was assumed.

**This is a foundational skill:** Everything will be built from this. The contribution and accuracy of the AI partner are very important. Space is left for the AI to reflect, judge structure, and do its best work. The constraint is on accuracy of record, not on how the work is approached.
