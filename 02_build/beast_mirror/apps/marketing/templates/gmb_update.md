# Google My Business Update Template

## Structure

```
[1-2 sentences — What's happening / useful tip]

[Call to action — Call us, book online, visit our website]

[Service area mention if relevant]
```

## Requirements
- 50-100 words maximum
- Short, actionable, local-focused
- Always include a CTA
- Mention service area naturally
- No hashtags (GMB doesn't use them)
- Plain text — no markdown formatting

## Update Types

### Offer
"Spring boiler service — book before [date] and we'll include a free
radiator bleed. Covering Jesmond, Gosforth, and Heaton.
Call [phone] or book online."

### Tip
"Quick winter tip: know where your stopcock is before you need it.
Most Newcastle homes have it under the kitchen sink. Turn it clockwise
to shut off your water in an emergency. Need help? Give us a call."

### Seasonal
"Autumn's here — time to get your heating checked before the cold
sets in. We're booking boiler services across Jesmond and Gosforth
this month. Ring [phone] to book in."

### Community
"Great to see Jesmond Dene looking brilliant this weekend. If you're
a local business and need plumbing sorted before the busy season,
we're here. Supporting Jesmond since [year]."

## Tone
- Direct and useful
- Local — mention the area
- Warm but professional
- CTA feels like an invitation, not a demand
