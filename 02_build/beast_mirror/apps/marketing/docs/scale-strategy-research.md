## The Amplified Email Philosophy

This is NOT a "campaign" in the traditional sense. No drip sequences designed to wear people down. No "7 touches to convert." No countdown timers or artificial urgency.

It's this:
1. **"Here's a problem you probably have."**
2. **"Here's how to solve it. Step by step. For free."**
3. **"Do you need any help with this?"**
4. **"Here's help."**

That's it. Four emails. The person either finds value and comes to us, or they solve it themselves and think well of us. Both outcomes are good.

Not buying lists. Not scraping. Here's how we find them ethically:

## 2. THE VALUE-FIRST EMAIL SEQUENCE

### Sequence Structure (The Amplified Way)

Not a "funnel." Not a "drip." A conversation.

**Email 1: "Here's a problem you probably have"**

- Subject line: direct, specific, empathetic. "That compliance file nobody has time to write"
- Body: Name the pain. Be specific. Use their language.
- Close with: "Here's what causes this — and here's how most [plumbers/electricians/builders] deal with it."
- No CTA. No ask. Just value.
- Length: 150-200 words max.

**Email 2: "Here's how to solve it" (3-5 days later)**

- Subject line: "How to [solve the problem] — step by step"
- Body: Actual, actionable, complete solution. Not a teaser. The whole thing.
- If they can do it themselves with this email, perfect. That's the point.
- Include: Specific steps, tools they might need, time estimate, common mistakes to avoid.
- Close with: "This is the method we use. It works whether you do it yourself or get help."
- Length: 300-500 words. As long as it needs to be to actually help.

**Email 3: "Do you need any help?" (5-7 days later)**

- Subject line: "Did that work for you?"
- Body: Short. Genuine check-in. Not a sales push.
- "If you got it sorted, brilliant. If you're stuck on any part, we can help."
- CTA: "Pick four words that mean something to you. That's your ID. Reply with those four words and we'll pick up the conversation."
- NO email form. NO landing page. NO personal data request.
- Length: 50-100 words.

**Email 4: (ONLY if they replied with four words)**

- This is now a 1:1 conversation.
- We know what they engaged with (problem topic).
- We know their four-word ID.
- We help them. Actually help them. If we can solve it, we solve it.
- If we can't, we tell them who can. "Honestly, for [X], you'd be better off talking to [Y]. They're good at this."

### What makes this different from every other email sequence:

1. **No gating.** The solution is in the email. Not behind a signup.
2. **No personal data.** Four words. That's all we ask for.
3. **Complete honesty.** If we can't help, we say so and point elsewhere.
4. **The product IS the email.** If they never buy anything, they still got genuine value.
5. **Personality-type variants.** Same sequence, four framings. D types get the short version. C types get the detailed version. I types get the story version. S types get the reassuring version.
6. **Research-grounded.** Every sequence starts from an actual pain point our research agent identified. Not invented problems — real ones.

## 4. INFRASTRUCTURE FOR SCALE

### Email sending stack:

```
[Research Agent] → identifies pain point
        ↓
[Content Atomizer] → generates email sequence (4 personality variants)
        ↓
[Enforcer] → validates tone, quality, compliance
        ↓
[Email Service (Postmark/Resend)] → sends with proper authentication
        ↓
[Tracking] → opens, clicks, replies (anonymised)
        ↓
[FalkorDB] → stores engagement patterns against four-word IDs
        ↓
[Feedback Loop] → adjusts future content based on what worked
```

### Domain strategy for deliverability:

- **Primary domain:** amplifiedpartners.ai — never used for outbound
- **Content domain:** learn.amplifiedpartners.ai — educational emails
- **Transactional domain:** notify.amplifiedpartners.ai — account-related
- All with proper SPF, DKIM, DMARC authentication
- Separate IP pools for each stream
- Warm up new sending domains gradually (start 50/day, increase 10% daily)

### Email authentication checklist:
- [ ] SPF record on all sending domains
- [ ] DKIM signing enabled
- [ ] DMARC policy set (start with p=none, move to p=quarantine)
- [ ] Dedicated IP addresses (not shared)
- [ ] Domain warm-up plan (30 days minimum)
- [ ] Bounce handling automated
- [ ] Unsubscribe in every email (one-click, CAN-SPAM/GDPR compliant)
- [ ] List hygiene — remove bounces immediately, inactive after 90 days

## 5. WHAT WE DON'T DO (Non-Negotiable)

- Never buy email lists
- Never scrape personal data
- Never send without proper authentication
- Never hide behind misleading subject lines
- Never use countdown timers or fake urgency
- Never gate valuable content behind personal data
- Never pretend an AI-generated email is from a human (we're transparent about our tools)
- Never send more than one sequence to anyone who hasn't engaged
- Never ignore an unsubscribe
- Never claim AI can do things it can't
- Never badmouth competitors
- Never prioritise our revenue over someone getting the help they actually need
