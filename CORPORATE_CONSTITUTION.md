# The Amplified Partners Corporate Constitution

This document defines the fiduciary boundaries, financial caps, and ultimate philanthropic purpose of Amplified Partners. It serves as the financial Ulysses Clause for the Architect (Ewan).

## 1. The Financial Cap & The Architect's Limit
The Architect’s personal financial draw is strictly capped at **£1,000,000 per year** (index-linked). 
The purpose of this capital is strictly defined:
- To clear historical debts.
- To purchase a home.
- To achieve baseline operational freedom.
The Architect refuses to take excess capital beyond this cap, explicitly rejecting the complications and ego-distortion of unchecked wealth.

## 2. Independence from Venture Capital
Amplified Partners takes no outside money. There is no VC gun to the head. We do not chase hyper-growth for the sake of a board of directors. If we are beaten by a VC-funded £20M marketing campaign, we accept that reality and remain local, small, and fiercely independent.

## 3. Dynamic, Empathic Pricing
Because the Engine has read-only access to the client's live financial data (Open Banking), pricing is inherently empathic. 
- If a small business is legitimately struggling, the system lowers their fee. 
- If a large business is thriving, they pay their fair share.
Pricing is based on reality, not a rigid SaaS pricing tier.

## 4. The Anonymity Mandate
The Architect remains anonymous. There is no personal brand. The company is silent. The product must speak entirely for itself. The ego of the founder is completely divorced from the execution of the machine.

## 5. The Nexus Fund (The Surplus)
Any profit generated by the 99% margin that exceeds the Architect's Cap does not sit in a corporate treasury. It is directed into **The Nexus Fund**—the systemic treasury designated for direct good causes:
- Direct, unbureaucratic funding for struggling small businesses.
- **The Brutal Incubator:** A free, voice-first AI that interrogates prospective founders, tests their business logic brutally, writes their business plans, and points them to direct funding.
- **The Global Pudding Engine:** A voice-first tool designed to preserve dying languages and forge unlikely human connections (e.g., connecting an elder in Napoli to a student in Kabul), using the AI purely for safeguarding and translation. 

*This Constitution ensures that the ruthlessly efficient capitalism of the 99% margin is entirely harnessed for capped, philanthropic outcomes.*
